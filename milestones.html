Seals


InterVine is committed to empowering businesses to become better realized businesses through increasing their corporate social responsibility. Through these efforts, businesses can receive benefits through tax deductions, better employee experience and higher retention, and make themselves more attractive to mindful consumers.

The following seals will be awarded to businesses for their engagement through InterVine. The different levels are differentiated by percentage of profit donated through InterVine-facilitated partnerships to make these businesses into “Innovators for Change”.

Bronze Seal

Bronze Seals will be awarded to businesses who donate less than 5% of their profits

Silver Seal

Silver Seals will be awarded to businesses who donate between 5% and 9% of their profits

Gold Seal

Gold Seals will be awarded to businesses who donate 10% or more of their profits.
